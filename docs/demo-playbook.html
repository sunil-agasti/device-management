This dialog appears on the target user's MacBook when access is granted:

** Admin Access Granted **

Hello johndoe, you have been granted temporary admin access for 5 minutes. Your privileges will be automatically revoked after the timer expires.

1 minute before expiry, the user sees:

** Access Expiring Soon **

Hello johndoe, your admin access will expire in 1 minute. Save your work.

Grant Notification: A native macOS dialog box appears on the user's screen confirming access and duration. A warning shows 1 minute before expiry.

When the timer expires, this dialog appears on the user's MacBook:

** User Privileges Updated **

Hello johndoe, your admin privileges have been revoked and updated to Standard User.

If you require elevated access (Admin Rights) on your MacBook, please request through the TCS Admin Portal.

Revoke Notification: After the timer expires, a persistent background process removes admin rights and shows this dialog. Works even after reboot — no way to bypass.
